[Developer Test Map]

This is a snake-shaped maze test map. Enemies spawn at the top-left corner and must weave through the winding corridors to reach the exit at the bottom-right.

The path features multiple sharp turns, providing ideal opportunities for area-of-effect and slowing towers. Make good use of the central platform and corner positions to maximize defense efficiency.

Note: This is a test level in the Custom chapter; difficulty and wave configurations may be adjusted at any time.
