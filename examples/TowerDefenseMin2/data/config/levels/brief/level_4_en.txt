Stellar Perimeter -- Mission Briefing

================================

[Background: The Deep Furnace]

The battle for the KX-7 system has entered its final phase. Full-spectrum scans from the Resonance Locator point to a single coordinate — the Federation frontier's largest Stellar Core Orb refining facility, codenamed "Deep Furnace."

This installation is not a single structure, but an industrial cluster of eight large mining platforms interconnected by a complex network of alloy conduits. Each platform once served as a smelting and refining node for asteroid ore, and the channel network connecting them has now become a "highway" for Swarm advance. The facility's underground vaults contain enough Stellar Core Orb reserves to power the entire KX-7 system — the largest energy concentration the Swarm has discovered since the invasion began.

Federation Command has classified Deep Furnace as a "Non-Fallible" strategic priority. If this position is lost, not only will the outpost and starport lose power, but the entire system's defense network will collapse. Even more terrifying, if such vast energy reserves fall into Swarm hands, they could expand the KX-7 spatial rift to an unprecedented scale — and what pours through will no longer be advance scouts, but the Swarm's true main force.

================================

[Ultimate Threat: The Overseer]

While analyzing the harmonic signals at Deep Furnace's perimeter, Federation scientists discovered an unprecedented pattern: high intensity, regular fluctuations, with clear hierarchical structure. This did not resemble an individual. It resembled... a command center.

Engineering designated it the "Overseer." This represents the highest evolutionary form the Swarm has yet demonstrated. The Overseer does not directly charge into battle; instead, it hovers at the rear, broadcasting high-frequency subspace harmonic commands to surrounding Swarm units. These commands can precisely coordinate the action rhythms of different Swarm types — when advance scouts launch feints, when hardened units切入 the front line, when Strikers execute flank breaches — all orchestrated by the Overseer's unified control.

Most dangerous is the Overseer's "vanguard escort" tactic: it personally leads an elite guard as the vanguard of a second wave, deliberately creating a brief time gap between the first and second waves. While defensive fire is absorbed by the first wave, the Overseer and its escort suddenly切入 from an unexpected node, driving straight for the energy core. This multi-rhythm, multi-wave precision coordination has surpassed any known biological behavior pattern.

One scientist proposed a chilling hypothesis over an encrypted channel: the Overseer may not be the Swarm's "commander" at all, but a more complete "projection" of higher-dimensional consciousness into lower-dimensional space. What we face may be merely the tip of an iceberg.

================================

[Full-Spectrum Defense Authorization]

Given Deep Furnace's strategic importance, Federation Command has lifted all restrictions on weapons system deployment.

In addition to Machine Gun and Missile Towers, you will also receive command authority over two critical systems:

Laser Tower: Fires a high-energy particle beam that inflicts sustained burn damage on individual targets. Its advantages include zero projectile delay and infinite penetration, making it particularly effective against fast-moving Strikers; its drawback is a brief charge time between shots and lower efficiency against clustered targets.

Slow Tower: Emits a special harmonic interference wave that disrupts the electromagnetic coupling within the Swarm's liquid metal structure, significantly reducing their movement speed. This tower inflicts no damage itself, but creates longer engagement windows for other fire units — against the Overseer's multi-rhythm assaults, every second of slowdown could determine whether the line holds or collapses.

================================

[Mission Objectives: Deep Furnace Mining Platform Cluster]

- Deploy full-spectrum defense arrays across the alloy conduits between the eight mining platforms
- Anticipate the Overseer's multi-rhythm assaults and maintain sufficient firepower at key nodes in advance
- Protect all Stellar Core Orbs in the Deep Furnace underground vaults
- If all orbs are lost, the KX-7 system will fall permanently and the rift will expand beyond containment

[Tip]
The Slow Tower and Laser Tower combination can effectively suppress Striker rushes; Missile Towers should prioritize the center of clusters to maximize shaped-charge blast coverage; when facing the Overseer's vanguard escort, reserve at least one upgraded Machine Gun Tower as the final line of defense.
